Introduction

Making a design decision that backs you into a corner is common. This book contains code organization methods to mitigate that.

The goal is to build systems that have acceptable behavior over a larger class of situations than was initially anticipated. Or, evolvable systems than can be changed to meet new requirements with only minor modifications to the code.
